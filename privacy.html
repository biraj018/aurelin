Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

At Auréline, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Contact form data — your name, email, and message when you contact us.
  • Analytics data — anonymised information about how you use our website (pages visited, time on site, traffic source) via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies — small files placed on your device to improve your experience (see Cookies section below).

We do not collect payment information, and we never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send our curated weekly newsletter (you may unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email).
  • To respond to your contact form messages.
  • To analyse site traffic and improve the quality of our content.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Cookies

Auréline uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These include:

  • Analytics cookies — via Google Analytics, to help us understand how our content is used.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies — via Amazon Associates and other affiliate programmes, to track purchases made through our links.
  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences on our site.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the site.

Third-Party Services

Our site may contain links to external websites, including Amazon. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies. We also use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (traffic analysis)
  • Mailchimp or similar (newsletter delivery)
  • Amazon Associates (affiliate programme)
  • Pinterest (social sharing)

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to correct inaccurate information.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • The right to data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@aureline.com.

Children's Privacy

Auréline is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify subscribers of significant changes via email. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests: privacy@aureline.com
