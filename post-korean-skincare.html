Why Korean Skincare Works

K-beauty philosophy isn't about covering up — it's about cultivating skin so healthy it glows from within. After 30 days following this routine with the exact products below, the difference was undeniable: smaller pores, dewy texture, and that elusive glass-skin finish.

The 10 Steps Explained

The 10-step routine isn't about overwhelming your skin — it's a layering system where each step prepares your skin for the next. Start with the heaviest removers and work down to the lightest, nourishing layers.

You don't need to do all 10 every single night. Morning routines can be 4–5 steps; full 10-step rituals are best for evenings when your skin has time to absorb everything.

How to Start Tonight

You don't need all 10 products to get started. Begin with a gentle oil cleanser, a water-based cleanser, and a good moisturiser with SPF. Add layers as you go, listening to your skin.

Consistency is the real secret. Most people see a visible difference within 3–4 weeks. Commit to the ritual and your skin will reward you.
