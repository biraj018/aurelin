Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 2024

Summary: Auréline participates in the Amazon Associates Programme and other affiliate programmes. When you click our product links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at absolutely no extra cost to you. This helps us keep creating free, high-quality content.

Amazon Associates Programme

Auréline is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Programme, an affiliate advertising programme designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com, Amazon.co.uk, and other Amazon affiliated sites.

This means that when you click on a product link on our site that goes to Amazon, and you make a purchase, we may receive a small percentage of the sale as a commission. This commission comes at no additional cost to you.

Other Affiliate Programmes

In addition to Amazon, Auréline may also participate in other affiliate programmes from time to time, including but not limited to programmes run by beauty brands, jewellery retailers, and other lifestyle companies. All affiliate relationships are disclosed in the relevant content.

Our Editorial Commitment

All product recommendations on Auréline are based on genuine research, personal testing where possible, and editorial judgment. Our affiliate relationships do not influence our recommendations.

  • We clearly label all affiliate links with the rel="sponsored" attribute.
  • We include an affiliate notice in every article that contains affiliate links.
  • Our editorial opinions are never for sale — we only recommend products we would genuinely recommend to a close friend.
  • We only partner with brands and programmes that align with our luxury aesthetic and values.
  • We will always tell you if something is sponsored or gifted.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the United States Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines, Auréline discloses any material connections we have with companies whose products we recommend. This includes receiving commissions for purchases made through affiliate links.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we choose what to recommend, please don't hesitate to reach out at hello@aureline.com.
